# ClassPartner Phase 1 Marketing Summary
## Board Review Document

**Prepared by**: CMO  
**Date**: April 5, 2026  
**For**: Board Review

---

## Executive Summary

ClassPartner is positioned for launch as an AI-powered academic productivity platform. This document summarizes the Phase 1 go-to-market strategy ready for execution once platform development completes.

**Key Highlights:**
- Target: 5,000 registered users in first 3 months
- Pricing: Free tier (3 essays/mo) + Pro at $9.99/mo
- Primary acquisition: TikTok, Reddit, YouTube
- Launch ready: Pending platform completion (CTO ETA)

---

## 1. Target Market - Phase 1

**Primary**: College Students (18-24) at 4-year universities
- High assignment volume, price-sensitive
- Active on TikTok, Reddit, YouTube

**Secondary**: High School Students (16-18)
- College prep workload
- YouTube-heavy for education content

---

## 2. Pricing (Approved)

| Tier | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 | 3 essays/mo, OpenRouter AI, PDF export |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Unlimited, GPT-4/Claude, DOCX/PPT, priority support |

**Conversion Triggers:**
- Email at 80% free tier usage
- DOCX/PPT export gated to Pro
- Upgrade prompts after 3 successful uses

## 7. Launch Timeline

**Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-2)**:
- Finalize pricing with CEO/CTO ✅
- Complete social media setup
- Create content calendar
- Set up analytics

**Soft Launch (Weeks 3-4)**:
- Limited release (500 users)
- Gather feedback
- Start TikTok content (5x/week)
- Begin Reddit engagement

**Public Launch (Weeks 5-8)**:
- Public announcement
- Scale content production
- Launch YouTube partnerships
- Activate referral program

**Optimization (Month 3)**:
- Analyze conversion data
- Optimize top channels
- Plan Phase 2 (Pro tier launch)

## 8. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Free tier abuse | Medium | High | Usage limits, rate limiting |
| Low conversion | Medium | High | A/B test pricing, improve product |
| Competitor response | Low | Medium | Focus on differentiation |
| Platform algorithm changes | Medium | Medium | Diversify channels |
